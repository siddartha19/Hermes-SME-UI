The AI back-office that runs your store

Alera is a crew of AI agents that answers your customers, checks every reply against your rules, and escalates anything about money to you — on Telegram, email, and voice. One screen, no jargon, no terminal.

Try it now → Read the docs

Built for shops like yours

However customers reach you — Telegram, WhatsApp, email, reviews — Alera handles the back-and-forth and only pulls you in when it matters.

Shopify / D2C store

Answers from your real catalog

"Navy jacket in L — ships to Pune?" → Alera replies with the product, price, and delivery window, then sends tracking — on Telegram, while you sleep.

Google Business / local shop

Reviews & "are you open?"

A 2★ review lands and a WhatsApp asks your Sunday hours → Alera drafts a warm reply for your OK and answers the hours instantly.

Restaurant / cloud kitchen

Catering & order enquiries

"Do you cater for 30?" at 11pm → Alera captures the lead, checks your rules, and drafts a quote that waits for your price.

Salon / clinic / studio

Bookings & reminders

"Can I move my Friday slot?" → Alera proposes open times, confirms, and sends the reminder — no missed appointments.

Wholesale / B2B seller

Quotes with your guardrails

"30% off 200 units?" → Alera declines past your 10% limit, counters at 10%, and holds the invoice reminder for your approval.

Instagram / WhatsApp seller

DMs handled overnight

Morning inbox full of "price?" and "in stock?" → Alera answers from your catalog and routes the hot leads to you.

What Alera runs for you

One assistant, the whole back office. Every action is checked against your rules, and money always waits for your tap.

Support inbox

Answer customers in seconds

Reads Telegram, email, and DMs; replies in your tone about orders, sizing, and shipping — automatically for the routine ones.

Catalog & orders

Real product answers

"Do you have a navy jacket for men?" is answered from your actual catalog with real options and prices — never made up.

Returns & refunds

Money never fires on its own

Refunds and invoices are drafted and pinned for one-tap approval. R4: always ask before anything about money.

Follow-ups & reviews

Nothing slips

Nudges quiet quotes, replies to reviews, and turns repeated feedback into a task — inside your discount and policy limits.

Built as an agent org, not a chatbot

A manager plans each request, specialists execute, a rule keeper gates every send, and every run is traced with its cost.
